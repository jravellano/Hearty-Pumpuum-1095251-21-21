Last December 2025, meeting you here in Atimonan felt so unreal. Hanggang ngayon, hindi ko pa rin ma-imagine na magiging ganito tayo ka-close. At first, there were times na nahihiya akong makipag-usap or mag-chat sayo, pero still, I kept trying to find reasons para makausap ka. Remember “Kuya Vicjun Mug right?” Hehehe.

Thank you for showing me love in your own way, and I really appreciate it. Thank you rin for appreciating my love and believing that it’s genuine. I love you, mahal ko.
